Welcome to Samsung Mobile Academy – Powered by iLearn

Congratulations on being chosen as the first Samsung Mobile Academy Trainers. We’re excited to learn with you, build confidence together, and support you as you grow into this role. Use this page as your simple access point to everything you need: Start Here, Assessments, Modules, Wellness Check-In, Game Center, and Support. Enjoy your learning journey — intentionally, consistently, and with momentum.

How to use this page:
  1. Tap the section you need (or scan the printed module QR in your notebook).
  2. Open your resources (Learner Guide, Workbook, Presentation, Resources).
  3. If a link doesn’t open, refresh your browser and try again.
