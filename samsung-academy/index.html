Welcome to Samsung Academy

This is your official learning access point for Samsung programmes delivered by iLearn. You can open each module digitally (no printing needed). If file storage changes in future (Google Drive → SharePoint → LMS), your printed QR codes will still work because they always point here.

How to use this page:
1) Tap your module below (or scan the printed module QR in your notebook).
2) Open the resources you need (Learner Guide, Workbook, Slides, Videos).
3) If a link doesn’t open, refresh your browser and try again.
